# Special Offers

## Academic License

Students enrolled in a degree or diploma-granting course of study at an accredited college, university, or high school may be eligible for a free academic license. [Learn more…](./students-and-teachers.md)

## Classroom License

Faculty members of an accredited college, university, or high school may be eligible for a free classroom license. [Learn more…](./students-and-teachers.md#whats-a-classroom-license)

## Coding bootcamps and training schools

Commercial training schools may be eligible for discounts in the purchase of their commercial licenses for teaching. Contact us at [info@devsense.com](mailto:info@devsense.com) for more information. 

## Universities and other educational institutions

We offer 25% discount to accredited universities, colleges, or high schools for development of their internal applications. Contact us at [info@devsense.com](mailto:info@devsense.com) for more information.

## Graduation offer

Students that finish their course of study and are holders of a PHP Tools Academic License are eligible for getting a 25% discount on their purchase of any of our products. [Learn more…](./students-and-teachers.md#are-there-any-discounts-for-students-after-graduation)

## Open-source projects

Non-commercial open-source projects qualify for free licenses for a year (renewal is possible). This license will give full access to all our products, upgrades and new releases. [Learn more…](./open-source.md)

## Recognized developers 

Google developer experts, Microsoft Most Valuable Professionals and other recognized community leaders, evangelists and experts are eligible for a free license. Contact us at [info@devsense.com](mailto:info@devsense.com) for more information. 

## Charities and non-profit institutions 

All registered charities and non-profit institutions are eligible for a 50% discount for any of our products. Contact us at [info@devsense.com](mailto:info@devsense.com) for more information. 

## Start-ups 

New software companies may be eligible for our 50% discount on our products, provided they have been in the market for less than three years. Contact us at [info@devsense.com](mailto:info@devsense.com) for more information. 

## Competitive discount

Owners of competing products can qualify for 25% discount on new licenses. We do not list the competing products that are eligible, the discount cannot be combined with other discounts and it cannot be applied to an already existing license. Contact us at [info@devsense.com](mailto:info@devsense.com) with a proof of purchase to see if you qualify for the discount.
